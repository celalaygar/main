How to Use

Learn how to make the most of WixBook Domain Track

How to Search for Domains

Simply enter your desired domain name in the search field and select the extensions you want to check. Tap the search button to see availability across all selected extensions.

Using the History Panel

The History Panel automatically saves all your domain searches. Tap on any item in the history to perform the same search again. You can also clear your history using the clear button.

Managing Favorites

When you find a domain you're interested in, add it to your favorites for quick access later. You can view all your favorites in the Favorites Panel and remove them as needed.

Changing Settings

Access the Settings Panel to change the app language, view app information, and get help. The app supports 10 languages for your convenience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does WixBook Domain Track help me find domains?

WixBook Domain Track allows you to search for domain availability across multiple extensions simultaneously. This saves you time by checking many TLDs at once, helping you find the perfect domain for your project.

Is my search history saved?

Yes, your search history is automatically saved in the History Panel. This allows you to easily reference domains you've previously searched for, and you can clear this history at any time if needed.

Can I save domains I'm interested in?

Absolutely! The Favorites Panel allows you to save domains you're interested in for quick access later. This is perfect for creating a shortlist of potential domains before making your final decision.

Is the app available in multiple languages?

Yes, WixBook Domain Track supports 10 languages: English, Turkish, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, and Korean. You can easily switch between languages in the Settings Panel.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your privacy is important to us. All your search history and favorites are stored locally on your device and are not shared with any third parties.

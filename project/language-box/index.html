Frequently Asked Questions

How does Language Box help me learn a new language?

Language Box uses game-based learning to make language acquisition fun and engaging. Each of our five games targets different language skills - from sentence construction to listening comprehension - ensuring a well-rounded learning experience.

Can I track my progress across different languages?

Yes! Language Box saves your progress for each language separately. You can view detailed statistics showing your skill level in each language and game type, allowing you to focus on areas that need improvement.

Is my progress saved if I close the app?

Absolutely. Language Box uses intelligent caching to save your progress locally on your device. When you reopen the app, you'll continue exactly where you left off, even if you were in the middle of a game.

How can I reset my progress for a specific language?

You can use the "Clear Cache" feature in the settings to reset your progress for the current language and level. This allows you to start fresh whenever you want to review fundamentals or try a different approach.

Does the app work offline?

While an internet connection is required to initially download content and sync progress, Language Box caches content locally, allowing you to continue learning even when you're offline. Your progress will sync when you reconnect.
